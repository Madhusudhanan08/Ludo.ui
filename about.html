📜 The Lore

In a world governed by an ancient force known only as The System, warriors are summoned across the land and given a single command — capture the dungeon.

Each dungeon holds immense power. Whoever claims it earns the title of Dungeon Lord and expands their territory across the realm. But the path is treacherous — enemies walk the same board, domains shift the rules of combat, and only the strongest party reaches the center.

You don't fight alone. Your party of four — Attacker, Tanker, and two Crewmates — must cross 57 tiles, survive ambushes, and outlast every rival clan. The System judges all. Only one lord shall reign.

⚔️ The Roles
🗡️
Attacker
The vanguard of your party. The Attacker moves fast and strikes hard. When it lands on a Domain tile, it activates Cleave — the power to eliminate one enemy piece from the board entirely.
⚡ Cleave — eliminate enemy piece
🛡️
Tanker
The shield of your clan. The Tanker absorbs punishment so others don't have to. On a Domain tile, it activates Tank Up — making itself immune to Cleave for the next 3 turns.
🛡️ Tank Up — immune to Cleave (3 turns)
🧑‍🤝‍🧑
Crewmate × 2
The backbone of your operation. Two Crewmates support your advance. They earn bonus XP for completing the board and turn Domain tiles into Safe Zones for the whole party.
🔴 Domain becomes Safe Zone for party
🎲 How To Play
1
Create a lobby using /play. Share your room code with friends. 2 to 6 players can join using /join CODE.
2
Roll 1 or 6 to release a piece from home base. Roll any number to move an active piece forward.
3
Roll 6 to get a bonus turn. Chain rolls to push deep into enemy territory fast.
4
Land on an enemy piece to send it back home. Land on a ★ Safe tile to be protected from captures.
5
Land on a ◆ Domain tile to activate your role's special power. Use it wisely — Cleave can turn the game around instantly.
6
Get all 4 pieces to the center dungeon to win. The first clan to finish captures the dungeon and earns the title of Dungeon Lord.
7
Each turn has a timer. If you don't roll in time, the dice rolls automatically. Don't waste turns!
